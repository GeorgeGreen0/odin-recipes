Veggies Chicken Soup

Description

This easy-to-make chicken soup is perfect for a quick and flavorful meal. It requires no special kitchen equipment and is highly adaptable to your preferences.

Feel free to use any combination of spices and vegetables you enjoy. The resulting soup is not only delicious but also visually appealing with its vibrant colors.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 3 pounds of chicken drumsticks and thighs
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 3 beetroots, diced
  • half a pound of squash, peeled and diced
  • 7 ounces of leeks
  • 1.5 pounds of potatoes, diced

Steps

  1. Place chicken drumsticks and thighs in a large pot.
  2. Add enough water to cover the chicken by approximately two finger widths.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of salt to the pot.
  4. Turn on the stove to begin heating the pot.
  5. While the chicken is heating, peel the beetroots, squash, potatoes, and carrots.
  6. Wash and dice the leeks.
  7. After 20 minutes of cooking the chicken, add the diced beetroots, squash, potatoes, and carrots to the pot.
  8. Cook for an additional 15 minutes.
  9. Add the diced leeks and 1/2 cup of olive oil to the pot.
  10. Add spices to the soup: turmeric, chili pepper (or cayenne), oregano, and herbes de Provence.
  11. Continue to cook until the vegetables are tender and the chicken is fully cooked.
  12. Serve hot.
