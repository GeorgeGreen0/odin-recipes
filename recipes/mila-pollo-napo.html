Chicken Milanese a la Napolitana

Description

"Chicken Milanese Neapolitan style is a dish that consists of a breaded chicken fillet, covered with tomato sauce, ham, and cheese. It is a traditional Argentine dish and can be prepared easily.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (breast): 500 g (8 fillets)
  • Cooked Ham: 8 slices
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese: 200 g
  • Crushed Tomato: 16 tbsp
  • Oregano: To taste
  • Ground Black Pepper: To taste
  • Garlic Powder: 2 tsp
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil: As needed
  • Salt: A pinch
  • Eggs: 1
  • Bread Crumbs: For breading

Steps

    Preparing the Chicken and Breading

    This stage focuses on getting the chicken ready for cooking.

    1. Season the chicken breast fillets with salt and pepper.
    2. Place bread crumbs in a wide container.
    3. Beat the eggs in a separate bowl.
    4. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
    5. Dip the chicken fillets in the beaten egg.
    6. Allow excess egg to drip off.
    7. Place the egg-coated fillets in the bread crumbs.
    8. Press lightly to adhere the bread crumbs.

    Cooking and Preparing the Sauce

    This stage involves cooking the chicken and preparing the Neapolitan sauce.

    1. Grease a baking sheet with olive oil.
    2. Place the breaded milanesas on the baking sheet.
    3. Drizzle a little olive oil over the milanesas.
    4. Bake for about 10 minutes, or until golden.
    5. Place crushed tomatoes in a bowl.
    6. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, oregano, and olive oil.
    7. Stir well to combine the sauce ingredients.
    8. Set the tomato sauce aside.

    Assembling and Finishing

    This stage focuses on adding the toppings and finishing the dish.

    1. After 10 minutes, remove the milanesas from the oven.
    2. Flip the milanesas over.
    3. Top with the Neapolitan sauce.
    4. Add the cooked ham.
    5. Add the shredded mozzarella.
    6. Turn on the oven's grill function.
    7. Broil until the cheese melts and turns golden.
    8. Sprinkle with oregano before plating.
    9. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.
    10. Serve with cherry tomatoes and rosemary fried potato wedges.
