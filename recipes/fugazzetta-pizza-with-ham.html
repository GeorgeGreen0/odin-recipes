Fugazzetta Pizza with Ham

Description

Delicious and flavorful pizza with a crispy crust and juicy ham. This pizza is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 100 grams all-purpose flour (000 flour)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine salt, or more to taste
  • Large pinch of sugar
  • 6 grams fresh yeast
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • As needed, warm water

Topping:

  • As needed, onion
  • 80 grams cooked ham
  • As needed, mozzarella cheese
  • Salt, pepper, oregano
  • Oil
  • Grated cheese (optional)
  • Olives (optional)

Steps

    Preparation of the Topping

    This stage focuses on preparing the flavorful onion topping.

    1. Slice the onions into julienne strips.
    2. Soak the sliced onions in warm water for a few minutes.
    3. Place the soaked onions in a colander under cold water.
    4. Drain the onions thoroughly.
    5. Place the drained onions in a bowl.
    6. Season the onions to taste.
    7. Mix the seasoned onions.
    8. Add a little oil to the onions.
    9. Let the seasoned onions rest.

    Preparing the Dough

    This stage is all about making the pizza dough and letting it rise.

    1. Take two tablespoons of the 100 grams of flour.
    2. Crumble the fresh yeast into the two tablespoons of flour.
    3. Add the remaining flour to the yeast mixture.
    4. Add the salt and sugar to the flour mixture.
    5. Mix the dry ingredients together.
    6. Incorporate the oil into the flour mixture.
    7. Add warm water gradually to form the dough.
    8. Knead the dough for several minutes until smooth.
    9. Place the dough in a warm place.
    10. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and a kitchen towel.
    11. Let the dough rest until it doubles in size.

    Assembling and Baking the Pizza

    This stage involves shaping the pizza, adding toppings, and baking it to perfection.

    1. Deflate the risen dough.
    2. Distribute the dough evenly on an oiled pizza pan.
    3. Turn on the oven.
    4. Place the covered pizza pan over the stovetop burners (if applicable).
    5. When the dough leavens again on the pan, distribute grated cheese over the dough.
    6. Distribute chopped cooked ham over the cheese.
    7. Distribute mozzarella cheese over the ham.
    8. Distribute the seasoned onion mixture over the mozzarella.
    9. Bake in a moderately hot oven.
    10. For a crispier crust, start baking on the oven floor.
    11. Be careful not to burn the crust.
    12. Continue baking in the middle of the oven.
    13. Continue baking near the top of the oven if desired.
    14. Add olives to the pizza.
    15. Turn off the oven.
    16. Leave the pizza in the oven for a few minutes with the oven off.
